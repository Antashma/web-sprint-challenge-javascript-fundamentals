Describe the four rules of the 'this' keyword.
The four rules of this the 'this' keywords are:
    (1) global binding, where the 'this' will refer to the window,.
    (2) implicit binding, where 'this' will refer to the object before the 'dot' (ex. object.method).
    (3) explicit binding, where 'this' will refer to the object in the parentheis of the 'call' or 'apply' method.
    (4) new binding, where 'this' will refer the the new objected being created by the constructor function.
